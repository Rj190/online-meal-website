pizza

Pizza is a globally beloved dish originating from Italy, consisting of a round, flat dough base topped with various ingredients like tomato sauce, cheese, meats, vegetables, and herbs. Baked in an oven, it offers a delightful combination of flavors and textures, ranging from melted to crispy and savory.

raagi mudde

Raagi mudde, also known as ragi ball, is a traditional South Indian dish made from ragi flour. It is a nutritious food commonly enjoyed in Karnataka and other parts of South India. Raagi mudde is often served with various accompaniments such as sambar, chutney, or curries.
